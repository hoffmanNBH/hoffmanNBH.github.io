The Fife & Drum Corp

All army regiments needed musicians. Fifes and drums were used to regulate the daily activities of the troops. They signaled when the troops should rise in the morning and retire at night, when to eat, when to assemble, and to sound an alarm. Musicans were also required to perform most of the duties assigned to all soliders.
